Beads

Requirements:
  1. Magazine
  2. Thin stick/pencil/brush
  3. Glue
  4. Pen
  5. Ruler
  6. Scissors
Procedure:
  1. Draw and cut thin, long, isosceles triangles from the magazine.
  2. Place the base of the triangle on the stick and roll the paper.
  3. Once you have reached the tip paste it to finish the bead.
