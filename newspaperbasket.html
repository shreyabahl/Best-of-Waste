Newspaper Basket

Requirements:
  1. Newspaper
  2. Cardboard
  3. Scissors
  4. Glue/tape
Procedure:
  1. Cut a double newspaper page into half.
  2. Roll the newspaper into a thin stick, starting from the corner and going diagonally.
  3. Cut the cardboard into a 2 identical rectangles of desired size.
  4. Line the rolled up sticks along the edges of the rectangle, and align the second rectangle on top of them.
  5. Bend the rolls of newspaper upwards.
  6. Weave the rest of the rolls into the pasted rolls.
  7. Once you have reached the desired height fold and stick the base rolls.
