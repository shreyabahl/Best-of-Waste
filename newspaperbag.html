Newspaper Bag

Requirements:
  1. Newspaper/Magazine
  2. Glue
Procedure:
  1. Fold the paper vertically, and leave a one inch gap between the two ends.
  2. Fold the remaining one inch on to the flap and stick them to make the newspaper into a hollow cylinder like shape.
  3. Fold the bottom corners into small triangle of same size and stick.
  4. Fold the triangles upwards and stick them to close the bottom of the bag.
