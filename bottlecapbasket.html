Bottle Cap Basket

Requirements:
  1. 100-150 bottle caps
  2. 1 CD
  3. Hot glue gun
Procedure:
  1. Align 10 bottle caps on the edge of the CD and tick them using a hot glue gun.
  2. Place 10 more bottle caps on top of the first layer, each bottle cap should be between the gap of the bottom two.
  3. Continue this process till you have reached the desired height of the basket.
