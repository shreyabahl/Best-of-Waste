Aluminium Can Diya

Requirements:
  1. 1 can
  2. Scissors
  3. Permanent marker
  4. Pencil
Procedure:
  1. Draw straight lines, at 1inch distance, from the top to the bottom of the can with a permanent marker.
  2. Cut the top of the can
  3. Cut along these lines using a scissor.
  4. Fan out the cut strips, to make a flower shape.
  5. Curl each strip around the pencil to make design.
