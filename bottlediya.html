Diya

Requirements:
  1. Plastic bottles
  2. Cutting blade
  3. Permanent marker
  4. Scissors
  5. Glue
  6. Glitter
Procedure:
  1. Cut the bottom of the bottle along the line.
  2. Draw petal shapes on to the bottom that you have cut off.
  3. Cut along these line.
  4. Stick the glitter on the inner side of the diya, and decorate the outside.
