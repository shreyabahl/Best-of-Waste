Coaster

Requirements:
  1. Newspaper/Magazine
  2. Glue
Procedure:
  1. Cut a double newspaper page into half.
  2. Roll the newspaper into a thin stick, starting from the corner and going diagonally.
  3. Start from one end of the stick and keep rolling it till it forms a disk.
  4. Keep adding these sticks till the coaster is of desired size.
  5. Glue the end securely so that the disk does not open.
